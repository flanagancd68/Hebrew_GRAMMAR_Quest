11.6 Top 10 Hitpael Verbs

  1. פלל - (tD) to pray, make intercession (80x in the tD)
  2. הלך - (Q) to go, walk; (tD) walk about, move to and fro (64x)
  3. יצב - (tD) to take one’s stand, stand firm, station oneself, resist (48x)
  4. נבא - (tD) speak or behave as a prophet, be in a state of prophetic ecstasy (28x)
  5. חזק - (Q) to be strong, have courage; (tD) strengthen oneself (27x)
  6. קדש - (Q) to be holy, set apart or consecrated; (tD) show or keep oneself holy (24x)
  7. הלל - (D) to praise, sing hallelujah; (tD) boast (23x)
  8. טהר - (Q) to be clean, be pure; (tD) purify or cleanse oneself (20x)
  9. יחשֹ - (tD) to be enrolled in a genealogical list; (tD∞ as noun) genealogy (20x) 10 אבל - (Q) to mourn, lament; (tD) observe mourning rites (19x)
