Hebrew GRAMMAR Quest

16.6 1G

  • Remember the Rule of Sheva that prevents two contiguous Vocal Sheva/Hateph Vowels
    • NOT יְקְ, BUT יִקְ
    • Whenever we see the \(Pre \ =\) ְ יִ of the Qal Imperfect, think “Rule of Sheva”69
  • The 1G will reject the Vocal Shewa in favor of a Hateph Vowel

To identify QI from a 1G verb:

  • Turn יַחֲ or יֶחֱ back to יִקְ
  • Turn יֶהֶזְ or יַחַלְ back to יִקְטְ
חזק ׀ חלף Strong
3ms יַחֲלֹף יֶחֱזַק יִקְטֹל
3mp יַחַלְפוּ יֶחֶזְקוּ יִקְטְלוּ

  1. Historically the QI \(Pre\) did not come the Rule of Sheva, however pretending that it did makes it easy to recognize QI 1G verbs.↩︎
