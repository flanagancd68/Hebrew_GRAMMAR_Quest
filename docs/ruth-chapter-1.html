    • 3.3 Silent Sheva
      • The Sheva is silent if the previous vowel is short
      • The first of two consecutive Shevas within a word is silent
      • A Sheva at the end of a word is silent
    • 3.4 Vocal Sheva
      • Initial Sheva is vocal
      • The second of two consecutive Shevas within a word is vocal
      • A Sheva under any consonant with Daghesh Forte is vocal
      • A Shewa after an unaccented long vowel is vocal
    • 3.5 Hebrew Diphthong = Pathach-Yod-Hiriq

      • 3.7.1 Furtive Pathach under final ח or ע is said BEFORE the guttural letter and is not a full vowel
      • Quiescent Aleph is silent, neither a consonant nor a vowel
